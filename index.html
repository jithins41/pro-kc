BECOME A FELLOW

Whether you are a techie from Bengaluru or an entrepreneur from Kolkata or a lawyer from Lucknow, we are interested in working with you and enabling you to have a voice in the political process. All we ask of you is a deep passion for politics and an unshakeable commitment to the Constitution’s core, tenets of Liberty, Plurality, Equality and Social Justice. You can engage with us by becoming an AIPC Fellow and choosing a local Chapter in your city to affiliate with.
